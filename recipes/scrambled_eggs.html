Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Oil
  • Feta cheese
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Heat a pan and melt a bit of oil in it
  2. Crack your eggs into it and wisk around with a fork until mixed
  3. Crumble feta cheese into the pan
  4. Mix until the cheese is all melted and the eggs start to firm up
  5. Season with a bit of salt to taste
