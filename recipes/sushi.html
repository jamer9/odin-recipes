Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Fish
  • Avocado
  • Soy sauce
  • Ginger
  • Vinegar

Steps

  1. Cook rice
  2. Mix a small amount ov vinegar into the rice
  3. Cut your fish and avocados into the desired shape
  4. Flatten your rice onto a mat and lay your fish and avocado on the rice
  5. Roll your rice around your fish
  6. Cut the roll into short cylinders
  7. Enjoy with soy sauce and ginger
