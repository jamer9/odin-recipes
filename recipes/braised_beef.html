Ingredients

  • Large cut of chuck or round beef
  • Beef stock
  • Wine
  • Carrots
  • Bay leaf
  • Salt and pepper
  • Potatos
  • Oil
  • Onions

Steps

  1. Pat down your beef and season with salt and pepper
  2. Sear in a large oiled pot
  3. Carmelize your onions in the pot, deglazing with wine
  4. Put your beef back in the pan, along with chopped carrots and potatos
  5. Pour beef stock over this, as well as some more wine and your bay leaf
  6. Put the pot in the oven at about 300 degrees F for about 4 hours
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste
