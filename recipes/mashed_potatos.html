Ingredients

  • Potatos
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Milk
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. Peel and cut the potatos
  2. Boil the potatos for at least 15 min, until they're very soft
  3. Mince garlic and fry it in a pan with some butter
  4. Mash the cooked potatos into the garlic, add more buter and mix until melted
  5. Add a bit of milk to reach desired consistency
  6. Add salt to taste
